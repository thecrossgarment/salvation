God's Gift of Love

A message of hope and eternal life

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."
— John 3:16

God loves you deeply and has a wonderful plan for your life. Here's how you can experience His love and forgiveness:

1
Recognize Your Need
We all have sinned and fallen short of God's perfect standard. This separates us from Him and leads to spiritual death.
"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." - Romans 3:23
2
Understand God's Solution
God sent His Son Jesus Christ to die on the cross for our sins. Jesus paid the penalty we deserved and rose from the dead, proving His victory over sin and death.
"But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us." - Romans 5:8
3
Receive God's Gift
Salvation is a free gift that cannot be earned. We receive it by faith - believing in Jesus Christ and accepting Him as our Lord and Savior.
"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God." - Ephesians 2:8

A Prayer to Receive Christ

"Dear God, I know that I am a sinner and need Your forgiveness. I believe that Jesus Christ died for my sins and rose from the dead. I want to turn from my sins and invite Jesus to come into my heart and life. I trust Him as my Lord and Savior. Thank You for Your gift of eternal life. In Jesus' name, Amen."

If you prayed this prayer sincerely, you are now a child of God!
